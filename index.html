How it works

From browse to ship in four steps.

  1. 01

    Browse the catalog

    Explore qualities by use, material, and weight. Shortlist what fits your line.

  2. 02

    Order swatches

    Request a swatch box to feel the hand and confirm the shade in person.

  3. 03

    Confirm specs

    Lock GSM, width, finish, and quantity. We verify the lot is in stock.

  4. 04

    Ship worldwide

    Your cloth is packed, tracked, and on its way — roll or cut length.
